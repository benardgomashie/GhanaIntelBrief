# **App Name**: Ghana IntelBrief

## Core Features:

- Article Aggregation: Aggregates news articles from various RSS feeds and news APIs focusing on Ghana business, tech, and policy.
- AI Summarization: Uses AI to generate a concise, 5-bullet point summary of each article.
- Relevance Assessment Tool: An AI powered tool evaluates each article to determine its relevance to money, policy, opportunity, and growth in Ghana.
- Source Curation: Curates and lists 2-5 original sources for each summarized article.
- Categorization & Tagging: Automatically tags articles with relevant categories such as Business, Tech, and Policy.
- Home Feed Display: Displays articles in a card format with AI summary, 'Why This Matters' explanation, and curated sources.
- Newsletter Integration: Allows users to subscribe to a newsletter for daily or weekly updates. Auto queue social posts. Optional manual review for first 1â€“2 months, then go fully automated once stable.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Gold (#FFB300) to represent Ghana's rich resources and opportunities.
- Background color: Soft Beige (#F5F5DC) provides a clean, professional backdrop.
- Accent color: Earth Green (#3CB371) to highlight growth and policy related news.
- Body font: 'PT Sans' for a readable and modern feel.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' for clear and engaging headlines.
- Crisp, professional icons that represent each category (Business, Tech, Policy) for easy navigation.
- Clean, card-based layout with clear separation of content for easy readability and quick scanning.
